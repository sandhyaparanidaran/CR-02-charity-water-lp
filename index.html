Mothers like Berkit from Ethiopia wish for their children to be educated so that they can have bright futures. Access to clean water not only gives children more time for school but it also improves their health, equipping the next generation with what they need to be successful.

Access to clean water helps girls trade buckets for books. Honorine lives in Madagascar where only 36% of the population has acess to clean water. Everyday, she woke up at 3am to walk 3 hours to collect fresh water. When her community received fresh water, she was able pursue her entrepreneurial dreams and become sucessful.
